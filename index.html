About EPICS

The Wolf Park of Lafayette, Indiana is in partnership with the EPICS Wolf team this year.

Wolf Park specializes in being an educational and research facility to help people learn about wolves and other animals in a safe and fostering environment.

Discover Wolf Park

Two projects: The Educational Center and Fox Enclosures were chosen

Teams were split among the entire team to seperate the work for the semester.

Community

Wolf Park makes all their money from merchandise sales and donations. An EPICS project would greatly benefit this organization.

Environment

Wolf park tries to keep the wolves and foxes in the most natural environments possible, thus a fox enclosure project would have to keep this in mind when executed by the EPICS team.

03

Education

Wolf park also specializes in wolf,fox, and many other animal education to people all around Indiana. Projects done by the EPICS team would have to keep the education of the people as one of the highest priorities.
